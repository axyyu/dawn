What is Coresearch?

A search engine built for students by students. Our goal has been to provide easy access to scientific and humanitarian resources to students that allows them to do what they do best: learn.

Features

Streamlined Research

  • Search through multiple databases at once
  • Read quick summaries for each article

Intelligent Keywords

  • See topics related to your search term
  • View concepts discussed in every article

Automatic Citations

  • Add articles to your bibliography during the search
  • Add citations in MLA, APA, and Chicago formats

Personal Projects

  • Create projects with a list of articles
  • Generate a bibliography that includes all the sources used
